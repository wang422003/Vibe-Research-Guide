<h1 align="center">Vibe Research Guide<br>Surveys</h1>

> Survey papers help you quickly build a panoramic understanding of **Vibe Research**: from the evolving role of LLMs in scientific discovery, to the full lifecycle of AI Scientists, to system design, benchmarks, and ethics. Beginners should **read these in order** to build a mental model of the field in 1–2 days.

<section id="survey-papers"></section>

## Paper List

<section id="survey-reading-tips"></section>

## Reading Tips

1. **Paper #1** is the best starting point — it provides a clear evolution framework from "tool" to "scientist," giving you a map of the entire field.
2. **Paper #2** adds end-to-end process detail: from hypothesis discovery to paper writing and peer review. Great for understanding "what a complete research agent should do."
3. **Paper #3** is best revisited when you're ready to build — its system design and benchmark discussions have high engineering reference value.
4. **Paper #4** is for readers interested in agent architecture design — the patterns are directly transferable to research agent systems.
5. **Paper #5**, though published in 2023, remains the best reference for understanding agent components.
